To embed a link in your text insert <a href="linkgoeshere.com">Display text here!</a>
Example:
 Hello, my name is bob and you must <a href="getafreevirusnow.com">click here</a> or you will die!


To embed an image in your text insert <img src="https://files.randomImageLocation.jpg">
Example:
 Take a look at this bird! <img src="https://files.allaboutbirds.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/prow-featured.jpg"> Isn't it neat?

To make a piece of text bold, surround it in <b> and </b>
Example:
 This is <b>bold</b>, yay!
