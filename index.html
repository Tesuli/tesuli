I am Tesuli, one of the many DJ's of DSI. My goal is to make music just as good as the old days, but without using the same old samples or voice tracks. We live in the present, and if they were able to make such awe-inspiring samples as Anasthasia in the 90's, why can't we make something just as incredible today?
